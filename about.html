Built from Real-World Experience

Design & Cart isn't a startup experiment — it's a productized workflow born from running a successful 3D visualization studio. After delivering 800+ projects, producing 25,000+ renders, and collaborating with 100+ interior designers, we identified a clear pattern: creativity was held back by operational friction.

From vendor delays and broken product links to disjointed tools and design-to-reality mismatches, designers were spending too much time managing chaos instead of designing. So we built the solution we wished existed.

OUR BELIEF

  • We design tools that integrate seamlessly into the creative process — not ones that disrupt it.
  • Every feature is built to support, not interrupt, the natural flow of interior design.
  • By simplifying sourcing, rendering, and ordering, we reduce the operational load on designers.
  • Our focus is on returning what matters most to designers: Time to explore ideas and Freedom to focus purely on design.
  • We’re creating a platform that makes your workflow feel lighter, faster, and more intuitive.

OUR APPROACH

  • We're not starting from zero — we have an active revenue stream and a proven client base.
  • We combine design tools, 3D-as-a-service, real commerce, and collaboration in one platform.
  • Built specifically for independent designers and small studios, not large execution firms.
  • Every feature is shaped by hands-on experience from 800+ real projects.
  • Our focus: remove friction, not add more tools to your workflow.

THE TEAM

  • Our team includes former designers, visualization experts, and project managers — not just developers.
  • We've lived the frustrations of managing files, vendors, feedback loops, and procurement firsthand.
  • This platform is shaped by real-world pain, not assumptions or theories.
  • We design with empathy and respect for the creative process.
  • Design & Cart is the tool we wished we had when we were running the studio.
