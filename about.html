We Saw Designers Drowning in Logistics

After delivering 25,000+ 3D renders to over 100 interior designers, we witnessed the same pattern: incredible creativity getting buried under spreadsheets, vendor calls, and scattered communication.

Designers were spending more time managing chaos than designing. So we built Design&Cart—the platform we wished existed.

OUR BELIEF

  • To create a platform that feels like a natural extension of a designer's creative process.
  • To eliminate the operational busywork that drains time and energy.
  • By integrating 3D visuals, sourcing, and client portals, we give designers back their most valuable assets.
  • More time to focus on design.
  • More freedom to bring ambitious ideas to life.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

  • We are championing interior designers and design studios.
  • Technology should empower creativity, not complicate it.
  • Our mission is to streamline the path from concept to delivery.
  • Every feature we build is guided by one question: "Does this make a designer's life simpler?"
  • We obsess over workflow, so you can obsess over design.

THE TEAM

  • Our team is made up of engineers, designers, and visualization experts.
  • We've personally experienced the frustrations of managing disconnected tools and workflows.
  • This platform is shaped by years of direct industry experience, not just code.
  • We are building the tool we always wished we had.
  • Design & Cart is our answer to the chaos of the creative process.
