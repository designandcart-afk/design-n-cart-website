The Core Workflow: Design Meets Delivery

This is the heart of Design & Cart—a simple, powerful flow that connects your design vision to real, purchasable products.

1. Select Real Products

Browse a curated catalog of furniture, lighting, and décor from trusted brands.

2. Visualize in 3D

Turn your selections into stunning, photorealistic renders that get approved faster.

3. Source & Purchase

Convert approved designs into purchase orders with a single click. No more spreadsheets.

Manage Projects, Not Paperwork
