From Render to Reality

A streamlined workflow that connects professional 3D visualization with real product purchasing — no more design-to-reality mismatch.

1. Submit Your Visualization Request

Upload your floor plans, design references, material preferences, and project requirements. Tell us about the space you're designing and your vision.

2. Our Team Creates Your Renders

Our professional 3D visualization team produces photorealistic renders using real, purchasable products from verified vendors. Every item you see exists, has pricing, and is available for purchase.

3. Review & Browse Tagged Products

Every furniture piece, lighting fixture, and décor item in your render is tagged with product details, pricing, and availability. Click on any item to see specifications and add to cart.

4. Collaborate & Refine

Use built-in chat to discuss changes with our visualization team or coordinate with vendors about products. Request revisions, ask questions, get instant updates.

5. Add to Cart & Purchase

Once you're happy with the design, add products directly to cart from the render. Checkout securely with verified vendors — all through the platform.

6. Track Delivery

Monitor every order in real-time. Know exactly when each product ships and when it arrives. No more chasing vendors for updates.
