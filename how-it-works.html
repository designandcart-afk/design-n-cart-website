From Vision to Reality in 6 Simple Steps

The complete workflow that eliminates chaos and connects your designs to real, purchasable products

1

Create Your Project

Set up your client's workspace. Add rooms, upload floor plans, mood boards, and references. Everything organized in one hub.

2

Select Real Products

Browse our curated catalog and select actual, purchasable products BEFORE rendering. Every item is real, available, and ready to buy.

3

Get 3D Renders

Our professional team creates photorealistic renders using the EXACT products you chose. Preview first, then get finals.

4

Review & Approve

Review renders, provide feedback via project chat, and approve designs. Clear workflow: WIP → Shared → Approved. Everything documented.

5

Add to Cart & Order

Once approved, products move to your cart. Process payments via Razorpay, generate invoices, and place orders—all in one platform.

6

Track & Deliver

Track order status in real-time: Placed → Shipped → Delivered. Get tracking IDs, estimated dates, and updates. Complete transparency.
