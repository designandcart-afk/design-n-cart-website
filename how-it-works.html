From Concept to Cart to Completion

A unified workflow that connects design, visualization, product selection, and ordering — no switching tools, no vendor chaos.

1. Create Your Project

Set up a dedicated workspace for each client. Upload floor plans, define spaces (rooms/areas), establish budget and timeline. Everything organized in one clean dashboard.

2. Get Professional 3D Renders

Our visualization team produces photorealistic renders using real, purchasable products from verified vendors. What you see is what you can buy — no mismatch between design and reality.

3. Products are Tagged & Linked

Every furniture piece, lighting fixture, and décor item in the render is linked to real products with live pricing and availability. Browse, compare, and finalize selections — all inside the platform.

4. Collaborate & Approve

Share renders with clients via a simple link. Use built-in chat and meeting tools to discuss changes, get feedback, and secure approvals — no email threads or scattered conversations.

5. Add to Cart & Place Orders

Once designs are approved, add products directly to cart from the renders. Checkout with real vendors, place orders, and get confirmation — all through the platform.

6. Track Execution

Monitor every order from placement to delivery. Know exactly where each product is in the fulfillment pipeline. No more chasing vendors for updates.
